The fermented food aisle is full of products claiming microbiome benefits. The critical distinction is between live fermented foods and pasteurised imitations. Heat-treated kefir, vinegar-pickled sauerkraut, and filtered kombucha contain no live cultures whatsoever. Here's what's actually worth buying.
